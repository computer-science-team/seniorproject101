The Software Tool Kit was designed by Timothy McClintock, Yousuf Ahmed, Roger Morris, Dharmik Pandya, Krunal Patel, and Cole Christensen during their shared Computer Science Senior Project class. This class demonstrates applications of all accumulated skills shared by the members of a certain group of Computer Scientists. The Software Tool Kit was designed in the Rowan University Campus of Glassboro, New Jersey.
